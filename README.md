ASU DAT490 Captstone Project

Analyze production of renewable energy over time and see if it affects electricity prices and CO2 emissions.
Use analysis and ML methods to predict future electricity prices and CO2 emissions.

Questions:
1. How does the share of renewable energy generation vary across U.S. states, and how is it
   related to electricity consumption?
2. How does an increase in renewable energy production (solar, wind, hydroelectric, geothermal, etc.)
   influence electricity prices in the United States and how are these prices expected to change over time?
3. How do the main renewable energy sectors (solar, wind, hydroelectric, geothermal, etc.) contribute to
   reducing CO2 emissions?
